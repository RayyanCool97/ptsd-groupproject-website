What is PTSD?

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. Symptoms include flashbacks, nightmares, and severe anxiety, which can interfere with daily life.

Symptoms of PTSD

Flashbacks

Reliving traumatic events as if they are happening again.

Nightmares

Disturbing dreams related to the trauma.

Severe Anxiety

Feeling tense, easily startled, or on edge constantly.

How to Get Help

Seeking help from mental health professionals is crucial. Therapy, support groups, and medication can help manage PTSD and improve quality of life.
